About Us

At Auto – Did - Acts™, our mission is to encourage self-learners
of all ages to acquire programming know-how through
self-directed learning outside of the classroom.

To support that mission, we created our bookstore, b00ks 4 sn00ks,
to provide new programmers, and their curiosity-driven mindsets,
with an array of analog books to help them develop at their own pace.
